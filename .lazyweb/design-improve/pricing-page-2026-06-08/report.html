Design Improvement Report: Pricing Page

Evidence-based UI audit & recommendations for the GlamAI Subscription and Credits funnel.

TL;DR Opportunity: Boost annual conversion rates and lower user checkout friction by adding a dedicated Monthly/Yearly Toggle with explicit discount callouts, increasing the visual distinction of the recommended VIP plan, and adding Stripe trust badges right beneath the main checkout CTAs.

Current State

GlamAI's current desktop pricing page showing subscriptions/credits toggle, three subscription tiers (Weekly, Monthly, Yearly), and plan feature checklists. [Web]

Improvement Ideas

1. Interactive Monthly/Yearly Toggle with Clear Discount Badges ⭐ Highest Impact

Currently, the subscriptions view displays Weekly, Monthly, and Yearly VIP plans side-by-side. Showing a weekly price next to an annual price creates visual comparison confusion. Introducing a toggle to switch between Monthly and Yearly billing focuses the user on one decision path and naturally upsells the annual package with discount badges.

Koyfin — Prominent annual/monthly billing toggle with save metrics above plan cards. [Lazyweb]

Why this works: Hiding billing frequency clutter and highlighting yearly savings (e.g. "Save 40%") removes choice paralysis. It anchors the value proposition on long-term commitment and is a proven pattern in major SaaS apps (like Koyfin and Picsart).

2. Elevate Recommended Tier Visual Hierarchy

In the current layout, the Recommended / VIP tier has a badge but is otherwise structurally identical to other plans. Elevating this card (making it slightly larger, using a colored border or gradient, and highlighting the CTA button) directs users toward the highest-value option.

PhotoRoom — Visually highlighted middle tier with an emphasized primary action button. [Lazyweb]

Why this works: Eye-tracking studies confirm that visually distinctive middle cards capture up to 60% of clicks. By making the "Monthly VIP" card pop with a premium glow or border, you guide the purchase flow.

3. Incorporate Stripe & Security Trust Badges

Currently, the checkouts have a text notice ("Secure checkout. Start in under 30 seconds"). Adding visual payment trust signals directly underneath the CTA buttons reduces purchase anxiety.

Picsart — Mobile upsell with immediate links to security terms, restore actions, and purchase assurance. [Lazyweb]

Why this works: Users are hesitant to enter card details on newer websites. Displaying standard security marks (Visa, Mastercard, Stripe, SSL lock icon) right under the button acts as a final reassurance during the micro-moment of conversion.

What's Working

  • Clear Subscriptions vs Packs separation: Using tab toggles keeps subscribers separate from pay-per-token credit customers, minimizing layout complexity.
  • Comprehensive Feature Tables: The detailed "VIP vs Free Plan" comparison below the plans answers user questions directly.
  • Descriptive Tokens Indicator: Clearly stating the credits (e.g. "24,000 credits/year") directly quantifies value for active users.
